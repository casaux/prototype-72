ASIC card verification

Your issuing body is who you applied to and received your ASIC from. It may be printed on the front or back of your ASIC card. If you're unsure of this, please refer to any correspondence you received regarding your ASIC application or contact your flight school.
Please enter the number shown on your ASIC. This is usually at the bottom of the ASIC and may contain letters and dashes. Please enter all the characters as shown on your ASIC.
Your CASA ASIC number is your ARN
Your ASIC issue number appears after your ASIC number a dash.
Please enter your first name as shown on card

Lodgement of photo

By giving your consent below CASA will receive and store your ASIC photo in the ways explained below.

  • It will be stored on CASA’s licensing portal for access by authorised CASA officers
  • It will be stored on CASA’s Medical Records System for access by authorised CASA officers and your Designated Aviation Medical Examiner (DAME). This will also enable a DAME to verify under CASR 67.170 that you are who you claim to be when being medically examined
  • displayed on your flight crew or air traffic control licence
  • otherwise disclosed in accordance with CASA’s privacy statement

By giving your consent below CASA will verify your ASIC and will also receive and store your ASIC photo.

If you do not consent to CASA using this photograph, you cannot download a digital view of your flight crew licence on your iPhone.

Consent to check ASIC and use photograph

I consent to my information being checked with the document issuer or official record holder via third party systems for the purpose of confirming the ASIC was issued and for Auscheck or any ASIC issuing body to disclose to CASA a copy of any photograph of myself that they hold.
