Download your digital view of licence

You can now carry your flight crew licence and remote pilot licence with you on your Android device using Google pay or your iPhone, iPod touch and Apple Watch using Apple Wallet – sign in to your myCASA account, view your licence details and follow the instructions to add it to your Google Pay or Apple Wallet.

Please note, for Flight Crew Licences it is not a substitute to your paper licence. You must continue to carry your paper-based flight crew licence as required by law.

  1. Sign into your myCASA account on your mobile device
  2. View your licence details
  3. Tap on the blue 'download digital licence' button
  4. Read through the guidance on using the digital licence and then at the bottom of the page, tap the 'Save to Google Pay' or 'add to Apple Wallet' button
  5. Your licence will then load into your Google Pay or Apple Wallet
  6. For Android users, tap on the details button for more info and ensure ‘automatic updates’ and ‘allow notifications’ is switched on.
  7. For iOS users, tap on the the dots at the top right of the screen for more info and ensure ‘automatic updates’ and ‘allow notifications’ is switched on.

Download your licence in myCASA

Use the QR code to confirm your digital view of licence

Anyone with a mobile or tablet device can confirm the details of a digital view of licence. It’s a quick and easy way to get access to any updates.

  1. Sign-in to your myCASA account
  2. Under ‘licences and authorisations’ tap ‘confirm details of a digital licence’
  3. Tap ‘scan the QR code’ button to active your device’s camera, place the QR code in the camera’s frame and tap the camera button
  4. Agree to the declaration, confirm you have the licence holder’s consent and
  5. Tap ‘confirm details of a digital licence’

The QR code functionality does not replace CASA’S verification process. You must continue to carry your paper-based flight crew licence when flying as required by law.
